Alight at Geomam and transfer to Incheon Metro Line 2 heading toward Incheon National University (인천대입구) direction.

Alight at Incheon National University Station (인천대입구역)

Short taxi to Binex Co., Ltd.

💳

T-money card works on AREX and all Incheon metro lines. Purchase at airport vending machines or any convenience store (CU, GS25, 7-Eleven). Tap on and off at every gate.

Getting to Osong Plant

By AREX + KTX to Osong Station

Most comfortable and reliable route — high-speed rail direct to Osong

~2h 10m
Duration
~₩30–50K
Est. Fare
1

Board AREX Express at Incheon Airport Station

AREX Express (공항철도) — Take the Express (직통) train to Seoul Station. ~43 min. Tickets from ₩9,500 — buy at the station or online.

2

Transfer to KTX at Seoul Station (서울역)

Head to the KTX concourse (follow "KTX" signs). Board any KTX or SRT train heading to Busan / Mokpo / Gwangju direction — all stop at Osong. ~35 min to Osong Station.

3

Alight at Osong Station (오송역)

Osong KTX Station is a major junction. Exit from the main exit and take a taxi to the factory. Taxis are available directly outside the station.

4

Short taxi to Binex Osong Plant

Show the driver this address: 충북 청주시 흥덕구 오송읍 오송생명5로 147, 바이넥스
(147, Osongsaengmyeong 5-ro, Osong-eup, Heungdeok-gu, Cheongju). ~5–10 min, ₩5,000.

🎫

Book KTX in advance at korail.go.kr or the Korail Talk app (English available). Trains run frequently — roughly every 20–30 minutes from Seoul Station to Osong.

By Local Bus from Cheongju Airport

Budget option — local city bus connecting the airport to Osong area

~30–40 min
Duration
~₩1,500
Est. Fare
1

Exit to the bus stop in front of the airport

Local city buses stop directly in front of Cheongju Airport (청주공항). Check the route board or use Naver Map for real-time routing toward 오송읍 (Osong-eup).
